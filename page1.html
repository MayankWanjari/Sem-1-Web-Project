Overview
The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred Hindu scripture, part of the Mahabharata, where Lord Krishna imparts divine wisdom to Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra. It covers philosophy, duty (dharma), devotion, selflessness, and the path to liberation.
Key Takeaways
  • Dharma (Duty): One must fulfill their duties without attachment to results.
  • Karma Yoga: Acting selflessly leads to spiritual growth.
  • Bhakti Yoga: Devotion to God is a powerful spiritual path.
  • Jnana Yoga: Knowledge and wisdom lead to enlightenment.
